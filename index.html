GymTime

Developed a high-performance fullstack CRUD workout and diet tracker built using ASP.NET that provides users with a seamless experience for managing their fitness goals and visualizng diet information in real time.

  • Implemented JWT Authentication and password hashing with BCrypt to secure my site and prevent data.
